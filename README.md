# Metro IT Solution

**Metro IT Solution** is a professional IT startup focused on building reliable, scalable, and business-oriented software solutions.

Our primary specialization is **Point of Sale (POS) software development**, where we deliver fully custom-built systems designed from scratch to meet real-world business requirements.

Website: https://metroitsolution.com/

---

## Core Product

### POS Software (Main Product)

Our POS software is the **flagship product of Metro IT Solution**.

It is **100% developed from scratch**, not based on any template, theme, or white-label solution.  
The system architecture, database structure, business logic, and reporting modules are all custom designed.

#### Key Features

- Sales & billing management
- Invoice generation
- Inventory & stock control
- Customer & supplier ledger
- Discount, VAT & tax handling
- Daily, monthly & summary reports
- Role-based user access
- Clean, fast, and scalable system design

This POS system is built for **long-term use**, **easy customization**, and **enterprise-level growth**.

## Secondary Product

### E-Commerce Solution (Supporting Product)

We provide **one e-commerce product** as a supporting solution for clients who require basic online selling functionality.

This e-commerce system is **not our primary focus** and is offered mainly as an additional service alongside our POS software.

#### Features

- Product & category management
- Shopping cart & checkout
- Order management
- Responsive design
- Admin panel for basic control
